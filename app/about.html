About Us

Coffee Place is a Neighborhood Café, inspired by the celebrated cafés of Europe.

Located on Nikola Tesla boulevard 85. We are introducing the modern European style of a social, neighborhood coffee shop to the people of Serbia.

Steps from CoffeeNirvana, and our Belgrade headquarters and training lab, this cozy escape from the urban rush offers our full line of craft coffees, as well as teas, sandwiches, salads, our house-made baked goods, and more. Beer and wine are also available.
